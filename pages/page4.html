  • How I Came to Know Thomas


Thomas Rumble is my step-father who married my mother while I was relatively young. My mother and I had to move around a lot during this point in out lives due to my father being in the military, which eventually ended with my parents getting divorced. Toward the end of this period of moving, my mother met Thomas, dated for a few months then eventually got married. I got to know Thomas sometime during the dating and I was quite happy if I can recall to have a father who wanted to be there for me.
